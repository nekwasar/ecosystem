Privacy Policy

Transparency in the digital era.

At NekwasaR, we prioritize the protection of your digital footprint. This Privacy Policy details the specific data we collect, how it is processed via our secure partners, and your rights as a user of the NekwasaR Store.

Identity Data

We collect your Email Address and Name to deliver your digital purchases and maintain your account history. We never sell this information to third-party advertisers.

Payment Data

Financial transactions are handled by Stripe. NekwasaR never sees or stores your full credit card number or CVC code in our local database.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used for:

  • Generating secure download tokens for purchased assets.
  • Automated invoicing and tax compliance.
  • Security monitoring to prevent fraudulent purchases.
  • Occasional store updates (only if you opt-in to our newsletter).

Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and process your cart. We also use minimal, anonymized analytics to understand site performance. No tracking data is used to build personal profiles outside of our ecosystem.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to request a copy of your data or its permanent deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the support portal.
