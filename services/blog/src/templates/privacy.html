Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

To ensure the integrity and security of our platform, we collect minimal but necessary information:

  • Device Metadata: We use device fingerprinting (hardware configuration, browser version) to prevent spam in likes and unique view counts.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses and access times are logged for security and brute-force protection.
  • Direct Information: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address and name.

2. Use of Cookies

We use cookies primarily for security and session management:

  • Authentication: Secure, HttpOnly cookies store your session tokens.
  • Preferences: Cookies may be used to remember your theme choice (Light/Dark mode).

3. Third-Party Services

We use Brevo for transactional emails and newsletter delivery. Your email is shared with Brevo only if you explicitly subscribe. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication, and rate limiting to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data or unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time. For such requests, please contact us via the contact form on our main portfolio.
